# Design a Complete AI-Powered Investment Analytics Platform in Figma

Create a complete, professional, enterprise-grade fintech product design in Figma.

This is a design project only.

Focus entirely on user experience, information architecture, design systems, visual design, interaction design, responsive layouts, and prototyping.

Do NOT generate code, APIs, backend architecture, database schemas, WebSocket implementations, SSE implementations, authentication logic, or technical integrations.

The goal is to create a portfolio-quality fintech product that can later be handed to developers for implementation.

---

## Product Vision

Design an Investment Analytics Platform where users can:

* Track Indian Stocks
* Monitor Mutual Funds
* Analyze Forex Markets
* Monitor Cryptocurrency
* View Investment Insights
* Manage Portfolios
* Create Watchlists
* Configure Alerts
* Analyze Market News
* Review Investment Performance
* Explore Investment Opportunities

The experience should feel premium, trustworthy, and data-driven.
